04 — Research

Currently publishing.

Ongoing Research 2025 – 2026
National College of Ireland · School of Computing

AI AgroCraft Robotics: A Neuro-Enhanced Architecture for Autonomous Multi-Agent Smart Agriculture in 2040

A novel architecture for fully autonomous precision agriculture that fuses three rarely combined research threads into one closed control loop: neuro-inspired edge inference using spiking neural networks, a Dec-POMDP decentralised decision layer for heterogeneous drone–AGV swarms, and next-generation mesh connectivity for sub-100 ms belief exchange. The system eliminates cloud dependency entirely, enabling real-time crop-stress detection and response at the field edge.

13–18× faster task completion vs. uncoordinated agents
15× less cumulative movement (energy proxy)
100% episode success rate across all fleet sizes

Key Contributions

  • Unified AgroCraft architecture where perception, decision, and coordination run inside a single closed-loop algorithm (Algorithm 1)
  • Formal Dec-POMDP decision model for heterogeneous drone–AGV swarms with a decentralised greedy task-allocation protocol (Algorithm 2)
  • Simulation study on 20×20 gridworld showing order-of-magnitude improvement over uncoordinated baselines across fleet sizes N ∈ {1,2,4,6,8}
